Frequently Asked Questions
1. How often should I service my AC unit?
It’s recommended to have your AC serviced at least once a year, preferably before summer, to maintain efficiency, prevent breakdowns, and extend its lifespan.
2. Why is my AC blowing warm air?
Common causes include low refrigerant levels, a dirty air filter, thermostat issues, or a malfunctioning compressor. A professional inspection can identify and fix the problem.
3. How can I improve my AC’s efficiency and lower energy costs?
Regular maintenance, changing filters, sealing air leaks, using a programmable thermostat, and keeping blinds closed during peak heat hours can improve efficiency and reduce energy bills.
4. What are the signs that my AC needs repair?
Warning signs include weak airflow, unusual noises, bad odors, frequent cycling, and higher-than-normal energy bills. If you notice any of these, it’s best to call a technician.
5. Should I repair or replace my AC unit?
If your AC is over 10 years old, frequently needs repairs, or struggles to cool your home efficiently, replacing it with a newer, energy-efficient model may save you money in the long run.
